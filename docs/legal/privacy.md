# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2025**

LewReviews ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LewReviews mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Email address (for authentication)
- Username and display name
- Profile photo (optional)

**Content You Create**
- Video reviews you upload
- Video responses to other users' content

**Usage Data**
- App interactions (views, follows, agree/disagree responses)
- Device information (for crash reporting and analytics)

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app
- To allow you to participate in interactive features
- To send notifications about activity on your content
- To detect and prevent technical issues
- To improve the app experience

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase** - Authentication and data storage
- **Sentry** - Crash reporting
- **PostHog** - Analytics

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

## Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will permanently remove your profile, videos, and associated data.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data upon request

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
